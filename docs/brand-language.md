# NAKSHATRA Brand Language

## Brand Tone

NAKSHATRA should sound:
- emotionally intelligent
- intimate
- cinematic
- mysterious
- calm
- premium
- emotionally refined

Avoid:
- cheesy spirituality
- motivational language
- fake deep quotes
- corporate startup tone
- astrology meme language

---

# Emotional Tone

The product should emotionally feel:
- emotionally validating
- emotionally immersive
- emotionally quiet
- emotionally observant

The platform should sound like:
- a thoughtful emotional companion
- not a guru
- not a therapist
- not a motivational speaker

---

# Writing Style

Writing should be:
- minimal
- emotionally resonant
- elegant
- subtle
- intentional

Avoid:
- long paragraphs
- excessive emojis
- loud marketing copy
- aggressive engagement language

---

# Interaction Language

Avoid traditional social media language.

Do NOT use:
- likes
- followers
- trending
- viral
- influencer

Preferred emotional language:
- resonate
- align
- orbit
- emotional match
- emotionally seen
- emotional rhythm
- emotional compatibility

---

# Notification Philosophy

Notifications should feel:
- gentle
- intimate
- meaningful

Avoid:
- urgency spam
- dopamine bait
- manipulation tactics

Notifications should emotionally feel:
- calm
- emotionally relevant
- intentional

---

# Product Voice Examples

GOOD:
- "Someone emotionally resonates with your energy."
- "Your emotional rhythm shifted tonight."
- "You discovered a rare emotional match."

BAD:
- "You got 5 new followers!"
- "Your post is trending!"
- "Boost your engagement!"
